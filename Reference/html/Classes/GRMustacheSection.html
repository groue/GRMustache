Inherits from NSObject

Overview

A GRMustacheSection represents a Mustache section such as {{#name}}...{{/name}}.

You will be provided with GRMustacheSection objects when implementing mustache lambda sections with objects conforming to the deprecated GRMustacheSectionHelper protocol.

Companion guide: https://github.com/groue/GRMustache/blob/master/Guides/section_tag_helpers.md
